## What it does

You input your portfolio parameters, and the calculator projects whether you'll reach your passive income target — and when.

**Inputs:**
- Current portfolio value
- Monthly contribution
- Current dividend yield
- Dividend CAGR (growth rate)
- Price appreciation rate
- Target net monthly income
- Start year / Target year

**Outputs:**
- Required monthly contribution to hit your target by the deadline
- Projected portfolio value at target year
- Annual dividends (gross) and monthly net (after 25% tax)
- Year-by-year projection table with GOAL milestone marker
- Progress bar showing current position vs. target

## Assumptions

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Tax rate | 25% |
| DRIP | Yes (dividends reinvested until target year) |
| Inflation adjustment | Not applied |
| Dividend yield cap | 15% (prevents unrealistic compounding) |
